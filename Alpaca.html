Alpacas



National Zoo

Fun Facts

  • Alpacas hum as a way to communicate
  • They don't have any top front teeth
  • Alpacas eat mainly fresh grass and hay
  • They have hypoallergenic fleece

Day in the Life of an Alpaca

  1. They wake up early before 7
  2. For breakfast they're fed grass and once they're done they go back to sleep
  3. During the summer around midday when it starts to get hot alpacas like to lie in the shade
  4. Throughout the day alpacas sleep and graze on the grass
  5. Around sunset is the end of the day for an alpaca and it's time for more sleep
